Empathize

Stakeholder Interview

Interviewing stakeholders provides helpful information about their context, allows us to identify business goals they are concerned with, and increases their support.
Stakeholder interviews drive successful stakeholder engagement and give me an understanding of your project’s landscape. This understanding can help us navigate obstacles before they arise and keep everyone engaged and supportive.

Competitor Analysis

Competitive analysis in marketing and strategic management is an assessment of the strengths and weaknesses of current and potential competitors. This analysis provides both an offensive and defensive strategic context to identify opportunities and threats.

Define

User Persona

A User Persona is a fact-based visual representation of a set of users that helps me visualize, understand, and build relevant connections with the target users. In the digital age, audiences are demanding user-oriented products. To respond to this kind of demand, me, as a UX designer, need to know who your users are and what they like to offer them an accurate service.

Hypotesis

The first thing to accept is that no matter how much research you do, your product is just a theoretical solution to a human need or want that you hope will result in business success. The hypothesis is your guess at why a particular solution will succeed. Once you succeed, and people are buying and using your product it’s no longer a hypothesis. It’s a fact.

Ideate

Userflow

User flow is the user's path to finish a specific purpose. It includes each step, from the starting point to the endpoint. I like to use userflow.com for this.

Wireframing

I draw overviews of interactive products to establish the structure and flow of possible design solutions. These outlines reflect user and business needs. I like to use balsamiq.com for this.

Prototype

High-Fidelity Prototype

High-fidelity prototyping is where a product begins to take shape. Using mockups with color and content, designers can create hi-fi prototypes that look and function as close to the final product as possible. I like to use Frigma for this or Sketch + Marvelapp. I can also use Protopie for exampes with close-to-real animations.

Design

Interface Design

Now, when we already covered all the important staff, we can just focus on creating beautiful UI design. Usually, I will start with creating one or few UI concepts for one main page, so we can discuss it and iterate on it. After approve of general styles (colors, fonts, etc.) I will proceed working on other pages/screens. I like to use Frigma for this or Sketch

Deliverables for development

Some of the deliverables will be already done on the previous steps, but some will still need some time to finish, such as Design system. Design system consists of two main parts: Styles (colors, fonts, shadows, spacings, etc.) and Components.
