About tixee

Tixee is all-encompassing trading platform

tixee is a multi-licensed and regulated broker offering a bespoke range of trading instruments on the award-winning MT4 & MT5 trading engines.

My Role

Product Design
  • Research
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • UI Design

Problem

Redesign

tixee recently updated corporate style (created a new logo, mood board, etc). My task was to make changes using this style to all their other products (portal, mobile platform, and marketing materials).

For tixee customers, the most important thing is the ease of use of the platform. We should not greatly change the structure to which regular business clients are accustomed. But we can offer them an alternative version of the portal (Demo version) with the possibility of switching between them.

About @tehbezzz

@tehbezzz works as trader in prop trading company. Being a trader, he has been using the old mt4 and mt5 interface for years

"I have only a few wishes. To improve the quality of my work, I want to open positions faster, and for this, the ability to open trades in 1 click is very important. Entering the price of the asset is very inconvenient, I want the chart to have more options for this. The interface should be very simple, only assets and the ability to open a position and nothing else".

Needs

  • Simple interface with interactive charts which would reduce trade opening time.

Pains:

  • The old interface no longer meets the needs.

Define

Hypotesis 💡

Web Portal

It is necessary to make two versions of the portal because the opinions of users are divided 50 by 50.

Web Portal

The ability to choose a price on the chart and not to enter it by hand is the most important part of most traders.

Mobile App

Most traders like the structural arrangement of mt4 and mt5, but they don't like the 20-year-old style.

Mobile App

Traders close positions on the phone more often than they open, they do not need a chart on the phone.

Dashboard

A trader's favorite assets should be easily accessible from the home page

Dashboard

Trader should see the Top Movers as well as his own positions on the main page in order to react faster.

Overall

Users of trading platforms love gamification. Ratings and bonuses.
